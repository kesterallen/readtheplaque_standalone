About PlaqueWorld

PlaqueWorld is a community-driven archive of the world's historical plaques — the small bronze, stone, and ceramic markers that quietly tell the stories of the places we walk past every day.

What is a plaque?

Historical plaques mark buildings, streets, and landmarks where something significant happened — a birth, a battle, a discovery, a first. They exist in every city and village, and most people walk past them without a second glance. PlaqueWorld exists to change that.

How it works

Anyone can submit a plaque they've found. Upload a photo, drop a pin on the map, and add a description. Submissions are reviewed before appearing on the site. Once approved, each plaque gets its own page and appears on the Big Map.
